# Target Job Description

**Company:** Stripe  
**Role:** Senior Data Analyst — Payments Product  
**Location:** San Francisco, CA (Hybrid)

---

## About the Role

Stripe's Payments Analytics team is looking for a Senior Data Analyst to partner with our Payments Product, Engineering, and Finance teams. You'll own the analytics layer for our core card and bank transfer products — helping us understand activation, adoption, and revenue patterns across millions of merchants globally.

You'll be the data expert who drives instrumentation, experimentation, and self-serve analytics for the Payments org. You'll also be a key partner in identifying retention and growth opportunities.

## What You'll Do

- Own end-to-end analytics for Payments product metrics: activation, adoption, feature usage, merchant health
- Design and analyze A/B experiments across onboarding flows, pricing surfaces, and product features
- Build scalable SQL pipelines and dbt models to power self-serve dashboards for PM, Finance, and Operations teams
- Partner with Engineers on event instrumentation and data quality initiatives
- Present insights and recommendations to senior stakeholders including VP Product and Finance leadership
- Define and maintain KPI frameworks for the Payments org

## Who You Are

- 4+ years of experience in data analytics, business intelligence, or a related quantitative role
- Expert-level SQL (we run on Redshift + Presto); experience with dbt preferred
- Strong Python skills for data analysis and scripting
- Experience designing and analyzing A/B experiments (statistical rigor required)
- Experience with BI tools: Looker, Mode, Tableau, or similar
- Strong communication: you can translate complex analysis into decisions
- Fintech, payments, or marketplace experience is a strong plus
- Experience building self-serve data products for non-technical stakeholders
- Familiarity with data pipeline orchestration (Airflow or similar)

## Preferred Qualifications

- Experience with Statsig, LaunchDarkly, or similar experimentation platforms
- Experience with event tracking instrumentation (Segment, Amplitude, etc.)
- Track record of autonomously driving data quality improvements
- Experience with Stripe's products as a user or developer

## Our Stack

- SQL: Redshift, Presto
- Transformation: dbt
- Orchestration: Airflow
- BI: Looker, Mode
- Experimentation: Statsig
- Event tracking: Segment
